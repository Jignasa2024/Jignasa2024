Welcome to Jignasa 2024 – GPREC’s Premier Technical Fest!

Step into a world of innovation and discovery at Jignasa 2024, an intercollegiate technical extravaganza hosted by GPREC College on the 24th and 25th of October. This year, Jignasa brings you an exciting lineup of events designed to ignite minds and inspire innovation, including Paper Presentations, Poster Presentations, and much more!

Whether you're a budding engineer, a tech enthusiast, or an aspiring researcher, Jignasa is the perfect platform to showcase your talents, exchange ideas, and compete with the brightest minds from across the region. Get ready for two days of intense competition, insightful discussions, and endless learning opportunities.
