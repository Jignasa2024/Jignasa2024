Kreative - 2K24

Kreative 2k24 is an exciting innovative idea contest where participants will work in teams to develop and present groundbreaking ideas on a given topic. Teams will prepare a PowerPoint presentation to showcase their concepts, with the option to build a prototype to further illustrate their innovation and strengthen their presentation. The event fosters creativity and practical thinking, encouraging participants to translate their ideas into feasible solutions.

  • Smart Automation
  • Fitness and Sports
  • MedTech/Biotech/ Health Tech
  • Agricluture, food tech, rural development
  • Smart vehicles
  • Clean and Green Technolgies
  • Smart Education
  • Disaster mangement
  • Toys and Games
  • Space Technology
